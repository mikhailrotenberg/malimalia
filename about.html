About Me

​I am professional Tattoo Artist living and working in Berlin since 2016. My main focus is to make high quality tattoos with the unique and exclusive design.​

Besides tattoos I am also continuously producing artwork available on both original canvas, paper prints and prints on a variety of accessories. I also organize classes to teach drawing.​

I have graduated the Art Faculty of Moscow University and specialized for one year in Oriental Art in China. My professional tattoo career started in 2011.​

So, if you want to get a great tattoo, I invite you to my Secret Garden Tattoo studio in the center of Berlin, in the cozy Prenzlauer Berg.​
